
# 🌍 Tourism Management Platform 🇱🇰

A full‑stack **MERN‑based Tourism Management Platform**  
**Course:** IT2080 – ITM (Year 2, Semester 2)  
**Institute:** Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT)

---

## 📖 Overview
Tourists visiting Sri Lanka often struggle with fragmented platforms for booking flights, hotels, transportation, adventure activities, and rental equipment.  
This platform centralizes all major travel services into a single integrated solution, simplifying trip planning, improving reliability, and enhancing user experience.

**Key Objectives:**
- Centralize tourism management services  
- Reduce dependency on multiple third‑party platforms  
- Improve booking reliability and transparency  
- Promote lesser‑known destinations in Sri Lanka  
- Support local service providers (guides, drivers, vendors)  

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 👤 User Features
- Registration & authentication  
- Search destinations and adventure activities  
- Book flights (aggregator/metasearch)  
- Reserve hotels and rooms  
- Rent vehicles (self‑drive or with driver)  
- Purchase/rent travel accessories  
- Secure online payments  
- Booking history & order tracking  
- Reviews and ratings  

### 🛠 Admin & Manager Features
- Role‑based dashboards  
- Destination & activity management  
- Vehicle, driver, and transport management  
- Hotel & room management  
- Flight listing & promotion management  
- Accessory inventory & order management  
- Booking reports & analytics  

### 👥 User Roles
| Role              | Responsibilities |
|-------------------|------------------|
| **Admin**         | System‑wide management, reports, moderation |
| **User**          | Browse, book, pay, review |
| **Transport Manager** | Manage vehicles, drivers, bookings |
| **Flight Manager**    | Manage flight listings & promotions |
| **Hotel Owner**       | Manage hotels, rooms, bookings |
| **Accessory Handler** | Manage accessories, inventory, orders |
